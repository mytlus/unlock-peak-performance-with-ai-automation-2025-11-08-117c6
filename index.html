About Twenty One AI

Twenty One AI Automation Agency was founded in 2021 by a team of visionary technologists and business strategists who recognized a critical gap in the market. We observed countless organizations struggling with outdated processes, manual workflows, and inefficient systems that held them back from their true potential. Our founding mission was simple yet ambitious: to democratize access to cutting-edge AI automation technology and empower businesses of all sizes to compete at the highest level. What started as a small team of five has grown into a global force of over 150 AI specialists, engineers, and consultants dedicated to transforming how businesses operate.

Today, we remain committed to our core values of innovation, integrity, and impact. We believe that AI should be accessible, ethical, and designed to augment human capabilities rather than replace them. Our mission is to build intelligent systems that don't just automate tasks, but fundamentally transform how organizations think about efficiency, scalability, and growth. We're proud to have helped over 500 companies across 45 industries achieve unprecedented operational excellence. We measure our success not just by the metrics we improve, but by the lasting partnerships we build and the genuine positive impact we create in every organization we serve.
